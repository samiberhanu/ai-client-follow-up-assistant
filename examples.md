# Example Workflows

This document contains fictional examples demonstrating how the AI Client Follow-Up Assistant can organize client notes and generate professional follow-up messages.

---

# Example 1: Missing Tax Documents

## Input

Client missing W-2 and January bank statement. Appointment next Tuesday. Need reminder.

## Output

### Missing Documents

* W-2
* January bank statement

### Client Questions

None.

### Priority Level

Medium

### Suggested Follow-Up Message

Hi,

Just a reminder that we still need your W-2 and January bank statement before your appointment next Tuesday.

Please let us know if you have any questions.

Thank you.

### Internal Notes

Appointment scheduled next Tuesday. Documents remain outstanding.

# Example 2: Appointment Reminder

## Input

Client confirmed appointment for Friday but still needs to send mileage records and receipts.

## Output

### Missing Documents

* Mileage records
* Receipts

### Client Questions

None.

### Priority Level

Medium

### Suggested Follow-Up Message

Hi,

This is a reminder that your appointment is scheduled for Friday.

Before the appointment, please send your mileage records and receipts so we can continue preparing your file.

Thank you.

### Internal Notes

Client confirmed appointment. Supporting documents are still needed.

# Example 3: Organizing Business Expenses

## Input

Client has restaurant expenses, gas receipts, office supplies, and vehicle maintenance costs. Notes are disorganized.

## Output

### Missing Documents

None.

### Client Questions

* Are there additional business expenses that should be included?

### Priority Level

Low

### Suggested Follow-Up Message

Hi,

Thank you for providing your expense information.

We have organized the expenses into categories including restaurant expenses, fuel costs, office supplies, and vehicle maintenance.

Please let us know if there are any additional expenses you would like included.

Thank you.

### Internal Notes

Expenses organized into categories for easier review and documentation.

All examples in this repository are fictional and do not contain real client information.
