# Scoring Guidance

A message passes review if:

* Information is accurate.
* Tone is professional.
* Assumptions are avoided.
* Sensitive information is protected.
* A clear next step is provided.

If the message does not pass, revise the output and repeat the evaluation process.

---

# Continuous Improvement

Document recurring issues to improve future prompts.

Examples:

* Messages are too long.
* Missing deadlines are not identified.
* Tone is too formal.
* Important questions are overlooked.

---

# Human Oversight

This workflow is intended to assist staff, not replace human judgment.

All outputs should be reviewed by a person before being shared with clients.

---

# Privacy Reminder

Avoid providing:

* Social Security numbers
* Bank account numbers
* Tax identification numbers
* Passwords
* Sensitive personal information

Use fictional or redacted information when testing this workflow.
